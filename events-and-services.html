Participation fees:

€68 adults; €48 under 18 years; children under 6 years are allowed for free.

The fee includes:

English-speaking guide on board and at Ostia archaeological site, navigation with complimentary snack on board, entrance fee to the site; the bus return trip to Rome will start from Ostia and will arrive at Porta S.Paolo. The fee does not include lunch.

Duration:

h 17:00: the guided visit to the Decumanus Maximus starts, along the following itinerary:

  • Via Ostiense necropolis;
  • 2. Porta Romana (Roman Gate);

  • 4. Sloping ceiling portico;
  • 5. Neptune's Baths;

  • 9. Forums Square, with the Capitolium, the temple of Lares Augusti and the temple of Augustus and Roma;

The itinerary lasts for about 2 hours, and it is 1200 meters long on level land.

Comfortable attire is recommended: jeans, sneakers, cap.

10th june 2015: PAPAL AUDIENCE

Price per person:

€ 20,00 base.

The fee includes:

admittance ticket and Lion Travel assistance.

Papal Audiences take place on Wednsday morning in St. Peter's Square (or inside the Sala delle udienze di Paolo VI /Sala Nervi). Audiences are not Masses, but rather moments dedicated to pilgrims and visitors to listen the Pope's words and to receive His apostolic blessing.They usually include a short and personalized greeting by the Holy Father in the language of the attending pilgrims. The Papal Audience starts at 10:30. Bookings must be submitted by 28th February 2015.

Info and reservation booking@liontravel.it
