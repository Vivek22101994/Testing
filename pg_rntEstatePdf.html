Morgante Apartment

Trastevere Area

Accommodation Description

Elegant holiday apartment located in piazza dè Renzi in the heart of Trastevere a few steps from two of the most famous squares of Rome, Campo dè Fiori and Navona square. The completely restored apartment is placed on the 4th floor with no lift inside a building of the 1600. It is made up of a large lounge with couches and a dining room, a large living kitchen, a double bedroom, a bathroom with a shower. It is provided with the air conditioning, a washing machine, a satellite tv, a dvd. The oriental style furniture, the wooden floors in each room, the excellent quality of the tapestry and the fantastic view of the roman roofs give the finishing touch to this enlightened apartment, unique among our holiday rentals in Rome.
