Maximum villa

Colosseum

 Enquire Add to my Wish list
Wonderful luxury villa located in Vicolo Antoniniano, a few steps away from San Sebastiano public park and close to Caracalla’s Baths, the Circus Maximum, the Coliseum, the Basilica of San Giovanni in Laterano and the Ancient Appian Way. Surrounded by a magnificent 3000 m² private park enriched by centuries-old pine trees and relics witnessing ancient Rome’s greatness, it is comprised of a large hall, two comfortable double bedrooms (one provided with en-suite bathroom with jacuzzi bath-tub), two twin bedrooms, a bedroom with double sofa bed, a spacious walk-in wardrobe, a dining room with fireplace, a fully equipped kitchen, three beautiful living rooms (one with fireplace), two bathrooms with jacuzzi bath-tub and one with shower.

A private parking for two cars, a fitness room and a sauna are at our clients’ disposal. All rooms are finely furnished with a classic style adorned by many pieces of art and antiques, and by the beautiful finishes and fittings, such as the gorgeous marble floors.

Amenities (including air conditioning, wi-fi, LCD TV, washing machine, dryer, dish-washer and safe), and the exclusive and well-connected location (Line B Subway station CIRCO MASSIMO is at 900 meters) make it an ideal solution for those who wish to spend an unforgettable vacation in the Eternal City.
