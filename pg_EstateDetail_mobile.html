This apartment is located in the Belle Arti Square, a short walk from the Spanish steps and Piazza del Popolo. The apartment is on the 1 floor of an Art Nouveau style building with a concierge service. It is comprised of a large living room and a dining room with a table for 8 people, 2 double bedrooms , a twin bedroom (two singles), a large bathroom with Jacuzzi for 2 people, a bathroom with Jacuzzi shower, a bathroom with shower and a big fully equipped kitchen. It's provided with air conditioning in all rooms, satellite Tv, dvd player, washing machine, dishwasher, hair dryer in all bathrooms and a fridge withe ice maker. On request, the landlady provides you the password for the Wi-fi internet connection (at an extra cost of €25). Its modern and antique design furnishing , parquet floors in all rooms and high ceilings complete the apartment.
